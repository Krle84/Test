Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Gastro & Stay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your information when you use our website or send us an inquiry.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you submit an inquiry:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Country of residence
  • Travel dates and preferences
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

We also collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type and pages visited, for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and provide accommodation information
  • Process and confirm your reservation
  • Communicate important information about your stay
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis

We process your data on the basis of your consent (submitting the inquiry form) and our legitimate interests in providing accommodation services. Where required, we rely on compliance with legal obligations.

5. Data Retention

We retain your inquiry data for a maximum of 2 years after your last interaction with us, unless a longer retention period is required by law (e.g. accounting records).

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law (GDPR and Serbian Law on Personal Data Protection), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@gastroandstay.com.

7. Cookies

Our website does not use third-party tracking cookies. We use only essential technical cookies required for the website to function correctly.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss or disclosure.
